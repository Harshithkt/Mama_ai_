# Mama AI 🤱

An AI-powered maternal healthcare assistant designed to support pregnant women through personalized guidance, health monitoring, document management, and multilingual conversational support.

## 🚀 Overview

Mama AI aims to bridge the healthcare accessibility gap for expecting mothers, especially in rural and underserved regions. The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Healthcare Data Analytics to provide real-time assistance, health insights, and communication between patients and healthcare workers.

## ✨ Features

* 🤖 AI-Powered Healthcare Assistant
* 🌐 Multilingual Conversational Support
* 📄 Medical Report & Document Management
* 🩺 Pregnancy Health Tracking
* 📊 Personalized Health Recommendations
* 👩‍⚕️ ASHA Worker & Doctor Connectivity
* 🔔 Appointment & Medication Reminders
* 🚨 Emergency Risk Detection & Alerts
* 📱 Mobile-Friendly User Interface
